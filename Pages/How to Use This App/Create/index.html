Tone Wheel

The core of this experience is built around our Tone Wheel system. Each audio 'set' is broken into 5 parts of the wheel. Tap the slices to play or stop samples. Play with different combinations and you'll find that no two sessions are the same.

Loading sets

Double tap the plasma screen outside of the Tone Wheel to load the set list. In the set screen you'll find a mix of ambience, deconstructed songs, natural sounds and music sets. Scroll up and down to preview samples from each set. To load a new set, tap the load button next the set title. While you preview samples, your current set will continue to play and will be shown as 'active' in the title.

Careful, loading a new set will replace your current set and change the currently playing audio. To close this screen tap on the top bar or load a new set.

Swiping sets

You can also jump to the next set by swiping your finger to the left across the Tone Wheel. Careful, swiping in a new set will replace your current set and stop the playing audio.

Each audio set has been carefully calibrated to match your listening environment whether you're using headphones, a stereo or the built in speakers of your device. All sets and tracks compliment each other. Please explore them. Look for more sets in the future.
