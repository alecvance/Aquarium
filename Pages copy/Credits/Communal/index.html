The Aquarium 2 project is an extension of our audio & visual Landscapes series. Our idea is to use emerging film quality digital cameras and on location environmental surround sound to recreate the immersive feeling you get from a trek into the woods or a hard-to-reach beach. Too often, we take these experiences back with us as low bitrate cell phone videos & photos. This direction called for the experience to be bigger and more surreal. This growing series of work has been developed into a commercial Blu-ray Disc series, stock footage and media installations.

We feel, that no matter how different, each project should be approached with a community mind set. This organic method allows for us and our clients to push projects further and find better ways to execute ideas. We want to hear about your project.
