Hints

A few quick hints to get you started ...

  • You can stop all audio by shaking your device. Shaking works anywhere in the app.
  • If you are currently in session with a tone wheel you can also play a track in the aural section at the same time (or vice versa).
  • On the create screen
    • swipe to your left to quickly change to the next audio set.
    • Double tap the screen to load new sets and preview samples. Tap the header bar or load a set to go back to the tone wheel.
  • On the aural screen
    • Pressing the shuffle icon when no song is playing starts playing a new random playlist.
    • Pressing the loop button will loop the current track playing.
    • The default playlist will play in an endless loop.
  • Each session will be different. Enjoy!

Look at the other 'How to Use' areas for more detailed information on each section.
